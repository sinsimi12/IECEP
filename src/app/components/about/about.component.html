Our Goal

The IECEP-TUPTSC aims:

    a. To promote and uphold academic excellence among its members.
    b. To develop a sense of unity among the students from Straight Engineering Program (SEP) to Ladderize Engieering Program (LEP) or Bachelor of Science in Electronics Engineering (BSEcE) together with Bachelor of Technology in Engineering Technology (BET-ELEX) students.
    c. To instill a sense of responsibility among the Technology and Engineering students of Electronics in serving the whole academic community in general and the organization in particular.
    d. To disseminate vital technical information in order to develop a well-rounded professionals.
