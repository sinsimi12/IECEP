Latest News & Events

IECEP-TUPTSC General Assembly

The General Assembly is held in the first term of the school year given that there should be a new set of officers.

IECEP-TUPTSC WEEK

The IECEP-TUPTSC week depends on when the University Student Government to which will they held its activity week. It is a weeklong celebration of the Electronics Department that will uphold sets of seminars, quiz bowl and Skills Olympic which are academically inclined activities.

ECE Night

The ECE night is the end celebration for the Electronics Department. Since it is a tradition in the Department, members of IECEP-TUPTSC are obliged to cooperate and join the event.

ABOUT US

Get to know IECEP - TUP Taguig

Institute of Electronics Engineers of the Philippines Technological University of the Philippines – Taguig Student Chapter was started in year 2005 it is the first organization in our school wherein Raymund M. Lozada, PECE, Engr. Danilo Miranda and Glenn Ortiz, PECE are the first advisers of IECEP. The IECEP-TUPTSC are one of the accreditated organization in Tup-taguig providing information that can help students with relates to academic and non-academic. The organization aims to uphold academic excellence and build unity among its members.
